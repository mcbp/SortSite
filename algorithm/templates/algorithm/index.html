Understand sorting algorithms

Sorting is the process of rearranging data into a defined pattern, and is one of the most common, important and fundamental concepts studied in Computer Science. Applications in the real world can be found across large varieties of types of software, including database manipulation, compression utilities and computer games.

This site aims to help you understand the concept of sorting and demonstrate how a variety of sorting algorithms function when applied to a problem. There are many different techniques and algorithms used to perform sorting operations, with each having their own strengths and weaknesses.
